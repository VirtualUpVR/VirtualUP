VirtualUP

Gorilla-style climbing, only up. Push off surfaces with your hands to move — no buttons, no sticks. Climb the 10-brick tower, then survive 55 unique floating levels — switchbacks, spirals, tightropes, walls, treetops, and moving platforms that slide, bob, and orbit. 150 levels. 1–14 climb the jungle sky — then a spaceship carries you to a second map floating in space with 136 structurally unique levels: monkey bars, chimneys, ferries, pyramids, mazes, tightropes, pistons, and more. No two levels play the same.
