VirtualUP

Gorilla-style climbing, only up. Push off surfaces with your hands to move — no buttons, no sticks. Climb the 10-brick tower, then survive 55 unique floating levels — switchbacks, spirals, tightropes, walls, treetops, and moving platforms that slide, bob, and orbit. Every level is different, and they get harder as you rise. Levels 1–14 climb through the jungle sky — then a spaceship carries you to a second map floating in space, where the glowing levels 15–55 finish the run.

On Quest 3S: open this page in the headset's browser, then press ENTER VR.
